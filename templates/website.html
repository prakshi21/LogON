NIT Delhi is an autonomous Institute which functions under the aegis of Ministry of Education, Government of India. It aims to provide instructions and research facilities in various disciplines of Engineering, Science and Technology, Management, Social Sciences and Humanities for advanced learning and dissemination of knowledge.
NIT Delhi has started its academic session in 2010 with three undergraduate B.Tech degree programmes in Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering and Electrical and Electronics Engineering. With three new courses, including Aerospace Engineering, VLSI and Artificial Intelligence and Data-Science.

NIT Delhi, established in 2010, is a premier technical institute under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Despite being relatively new, the institute has rapidly gained recognition for its academic excellence, industry partnerships, and placement opportunities. The National Institute of Technology Delhi (NIT Delhi) is one of the 31 prestigious NITs established by the Government of India. It was founded in 2010 and has since been declared an Institute of National Importance. The institute currently operates from its permanent campus in Narela, New Delhi.
